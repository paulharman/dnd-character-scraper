# Spare the Dying
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 15 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature within range that has 0 [Hit Points](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) and isn't dead. The creature becomes [Stable](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/stable-xphb.md).

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).
