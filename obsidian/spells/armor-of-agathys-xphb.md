# Armor of Agathys
*1st-level, Abjuration*  
![](/z_Mechanics/CLI/spells/img/armor-of-agathys.webp#right)

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a shard of blue glass)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Protective magical frost surrounds you. You gain 5 [Temporary Hit Points](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md). If a creature hits you with a melee attack roll before the spell ends, the creature takes 5 Cold damage. The spell ends early if you have no [Temporary Hit Points](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The [Temporary Hit Points](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) and the Cold damage both increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Warlock](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 243*
