# Aura of Purity
*4th-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot Emanation for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have [Resistance](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Poison damage and [Advantage](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end effects that include the [Blinded](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded), [Charmed](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Charmed), [Deafened](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Deafened), [Frightened](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Frightened), [Paralyzed](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Paralyzed), [Poisoned](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned), or [Stunned](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Stunned) condition.

**Classes**: [Cleric](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Bard](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Paladin](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 244*
