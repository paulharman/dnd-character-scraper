# Blur
*2nd-level, Illusion*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Your body becomes blurred. For the duration, any creature has [Disadvantage](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on attack rolls against you. An attacker is immune to this effect if it perceives you with [Blindsight](/z_Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Blindsight) or [Truesight](/z_Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Truesight).
