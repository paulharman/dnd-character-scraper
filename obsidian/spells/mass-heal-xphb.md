# Mass Heal
*9th-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A flood of healing energy flows from you into creatures around you. You restore up to 700 [Hit Points](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), divided as you choose among any number of creatures that you can see within range. Creatures healed by this spell also have the [Blinded](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Deafened), and [Poisoned](/z_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) conditions removed from them.

**Classes**: [Cleric](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Bard](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 296. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*
