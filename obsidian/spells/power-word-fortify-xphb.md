# Power Word Fortify
*7th-level, Enchantment*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You fortify up to six creatures you can see within range. The spell bestows 120 [Temporary Hit Points](/z_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md), which you divide among the spell's recipients.

**Classes**: [Cleric](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Bard](/z_Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 306*
